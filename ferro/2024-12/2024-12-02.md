Background Lateral patellofemoral compression syndrome (LPCS) is a condition characterized by increased lateral patellofemoral joint pressure caused by long-standing lateral patella tilt, adaptive lateral retinaculum tightening, and an imbalance in stress between the lateral and medial femoral condyles.
